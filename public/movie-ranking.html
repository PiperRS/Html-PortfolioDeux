The Best Movies According to Risa

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Lord of The Rings Trilogy

The story of Frodo and his journey to save Middle Earth will forever be my favorite.

Death Becomes Her

This dark comedy had me laughing since I was a teenager. To this day, I can recite every line word for word!

True Lies

Arnold and Jamie Lee make the perfect match in this spy thriller. Loved this movie since I was a kid.
The tango dance is an epic plus at the beginning and end of the movie.
